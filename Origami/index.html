Intersting Facts About Camels

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camels can move easily across the sand because of their specially designed feet
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible and store in their humps

Intersting Facts About Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication
  3. Most of them have a prehesile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Intersting Facts About Pigeons

  1. Were used as messenger birds during World War 1
  2. They are of the bird suborder
  3. They are very smart animals, and can recognize themselves in a mirror

Intersting Facts About Teddy Bear

  1. Teddy Bears are not real
  2. Little children often sleep with teddy bears
  3. If you cut open a teddy bear, you will find lots of fluffy white stuff

Intersting Facts About Pandas

  1. Pandas are an endangered species
  2. Pandas sleep for most of the day
  3. Pandas mainly feed on bamboo

Intersting Facts About Flying Cicada
